Why Use This?

Trashlocator is necessary for a healthy environment. Nowadays, trash is literally everywhere. One can take a look at the Hudson river and find soda cans, candy wrappers, and much more garbage. No one wants to ruin the environment for their future generations, right?

What This Does.

The user can track progress of waste removal from the street and is given the ability to provide input to the Department of Sanitation for the development of trends of where an abundance of trash and waste will be in the City of New York so that its removal can be better coordinated.

What makes this possible?

Thanks to various apis such as the google map api, we can locate trash efficiently. Moreover, this website is created using foundation, so the foundation developers deserve credit as well. Lastly, this is brought to you by Stuyvesant High School Software Development period 6. :)
